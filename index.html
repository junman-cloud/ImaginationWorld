Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our process

How long does a masterpiece reproduction take?
Quality takes time! Depending on the complexity and size, a hand-painted reproduction usually takes 4-8 weeks. This allows for proper drying time and layering of paints.
Can I choose a custom size for my invitations?
Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke designs, meaning every aspect—including size, paper weight, and shape—can be tailored to your specific event vision.
Do you ship international orders?
Yes, Imagination World ships worldwide! We use premium, insured couriers to ensure your artwork or invitations arrive safely and in perfect condition.
What materials do you use for your paintings?
We use professional-grade oil and acrylic paints on high-quality gallery-wrapped canvases to ensure longevity and vibrant colors that won't fade over time.
